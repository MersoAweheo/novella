PROJECT PURPLE PLAN: 

LAVENDER AVANA, AUNT OF MARJORY GAREN, WILL UNDERGO A PROCESS TO BE TRANSFERRED TO A NEW BODY. THE BODY WILL BE A DOLLY-TYPE CLONE OF HERSELF, BUT HAVE NO MIND AND BE ORGANICALLY GROWN. ONCE IT HAS REACHED AN AGE THAT WOULD BE RECOGNIZABLE BY MARJORY, LAVENDER WILL ENTER HER NEW BODY. HER PRIOR BODY WILL LIKELY BE DISCARDED UPON TRANSFER. IN HER NEW BODY, LAVENDER WILL BE ABLE TO GET CLOSER TO MARJORY AND DISPATCH MARJORY. THIS IS BECAUSE LAVENDER AND VIOLET GAREN (MARJORY’S MOTHER) ARE BOTH DOLLY-TYPE CLONES OF THEIR OWN MOTHER SINCE THEY WERE PART OF EFFORTS TO OVERTHROW ENTHO. IF LAVENDER IS IN A NEW BODY THAT IS YOUNG ENOUGH, SHE MAY LOOK LIKE VIOLET AND CAUSE GUILT AND CONFUSION WITH MARJORY DUE TO MARJORY HARBORING HATRED TO HERSELF SINCE SHE WAS NOT AROUND FOR HER MOTHER’S DEATH. MARJORY WILL SUCCESSFULLY BE SLAIN. THIS CAN SEVERELY DECREASE THE ONGOING THREAT OF THE LUNACY AND RUSSELL GAREN’S POTENTIAL SUCCESS. AS OF THE CREATION OF THIS FILE AND SUMMARY, NO EXPERIMENTATION OR BODY CREATION HAS TAKEN PLACE. THIS OPERATION WILL USE EXPERIMENTAL TECHNOLOGY FROM PROJECT MOBIUS. IF THIS IS A SUCCESS, AUTHORIZE PROJECT OVAL AND MORE IMPORTANTLY, PROJECT OCTAGON. 

PROJECT OVAL: 

OVAL TOWER, A FORMER FORTRESS USED BY ENTHO OFF SHORE OF GARENBURG, WILL BE REFURBISHED FOR USE BY VINCENT CARIS. VINCENT WILL USE THE TOWER FOR EXPERIMENTATION AND FURTHER CONTRACTS WITH THE SILVER BED FOUNDATION. THIS WILL ONLY OCCUR IF PROJECT PURPLE IS A SUCCESS. ALL TECHNOLOGY FROM PROJECT MOBIUS WILL BE MOVED TO THIS NEW LOCATION.
